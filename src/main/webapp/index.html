How our journey started?

  • Mid July

    Our Beginnings

    VDSI Hackathon July 29-30 2015 was announced on the 3rd week of July. Our journey started with the announcement.

  • July 27-28

    Code Avengers is Born

    We moved quickly to name our Team Code Avengers, formed a Ninja Team and completed registrations. We decided to create something useful and reusable, learning some new technologies as we progressed.

  • July 29-30

    Executing the Plan

    We started execution using the technologies we had decided upon. Implementing and Re-implementing new concepts as we progressed. The Idea was to put in our Best and Honest Effort.

  • August 3

    Innovating for our Customers!

    Now that we have the experience of participation, we want to encourage more of our team members to leverage this unique platform. A platform for us to innovate for our customers.
